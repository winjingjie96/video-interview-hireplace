Luckily for me, the team at NOC is world class in what they do and found me an internship in under two weeks. I am now interning at an early stage startup, Hireplace.

What Hireplace is trying to do, is to match job seekers with employers in a speed dating format, virtually.

I have tried to find jobs in the past and went through the whole process of putting your CV on job portals, sending your CV out to multiple employers and praying that you get a reply from companies. More often than not, zero replies.

Instead of that disappointing experience, Hireplace helps you to talk to multiple companies, in a one hour event.

Typically, the event will host 3-8 companies. You get to have a 5 minute “pre-interview” session with each company, where you introduce yourself and the company introduce themselves.

Face to face using video calls.

In just one hour, you can score multiple interviews and have a chance to make a lasting impression. Also, being able to expose yourself to multiple companies can help you compare the different qualities of each company.

We have organised two live events with NOC and the majority of students that joined both events were shortlisted by companies. We are now going into the second phase to host more events for every student in NUS.
