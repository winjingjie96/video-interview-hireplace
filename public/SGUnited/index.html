4 steps to getting a new job

  • 1. Explore hiring events

    Browse through the events that interest you. All events are virtual, invite only and have limited slots.

  • 2. Request for an invite

    Register for the event and if you are a good fit, you will receive an invite and instructions in your email.

  • 3. Attend the event via our app

    On event day, choose the companies that you want to talk to. Most interviews are 5 minutes but some could be longer.

  • 4. The shortlist

    Companies will shortlist suitable candidates and schedule a longer interview as the next step.
